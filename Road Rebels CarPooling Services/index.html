Road Rebels CarPooling Services

Despite the well-known benefits of carpooling, such as reduced traffic congestion, cost savings, and environmental advantages, the overall adoption rate among college students remains low. Traditionally,our service involved informal arrangements among colleagues or neighbors. Today, technology plays a major role, with carpooling apps connecting drivers and passengers with compatible routes and schedules.
